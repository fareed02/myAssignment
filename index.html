The First Day of School



Today is the day that we go bacl to school. My family excited! We all think thats cool.

We all know that we'll have homework and study like crazy. We won't have a chance to relax and be lazy.

We all wake up early in the morning. We'll find up our packs and hoist them and haul them to school on our backs.

I can’t wait to meet my new teacher and all of my classmates and friends. The first day of school is exciting, though it’s sad summer break has to end.

We all get to meet and eat cookies. We take assigned seats and learn names. We write about summer vacation. Then we play flashcards and games.

I really love summer vacation. Swimming and biking’s so cool. But still I can say that my favorite day is the very first day that starts school.

So, why are we having this grand celebration? Today we start planning next summer's vacations.

~ Kenn Nesbit
